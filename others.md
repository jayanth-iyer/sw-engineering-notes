# Curated List of Articles and Resources other than S/W Engineering

1. [Mathematics for Computer Science](https://courses.csail.mit.edu/6.042/spring18/mcs.pdf)

2. Stock Picking
    - Return on Equity - tells if it is a good business.
    - Debt to Equity Ratio - ideaally below 50% - tells if it is a safe business.
    - Price to Earnings Ratio - what you pay vs what you get - Price of the Stock - tells you if it is a good deal.
    - Low PE can be a trap, better if below 20.
